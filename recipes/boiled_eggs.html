Hard Boiled Eggs



Description

Yes, I know it's easy but I also know that it's not obvious

Ingredients

  • eggs as many as you want
  • water

Steps

  1. Place the eggs in a saucepan and cover them with cold water (about 2 cm above the eggs).
  2. Bring the water to a boil over medium-high heat.
  3. Once boiling, reduce the heat slightly and let them boil for:
    • Soft-boiled eggs: 6–7 minutes
    • Medium-boiled eggs: 8–9 minutes
    • Hard-boiled eggs: 10–12 minutes
  4. Use cold water to cold them down
  5. Peel the eggs by gently tapping them on a hard surface and rolling them between your hands.
