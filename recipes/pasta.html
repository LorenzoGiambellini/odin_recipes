Pasta



Description

As an Italian, it is my duty to teach how to cook pasta.

Ingredients (for 2 servings)

  • 200g (7 oz) pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • Water (enough to boil the pasta)
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • tbsp butter or extra virgin olive oil
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the water: Fill a pot with plenty of water (about 1 liter per 100g of pasta) and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add salt: Once boiling, add the salt (about 10g per liter of water).
  3. Cook the pasta: Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions, stirring occasionally.
  4. Drain the pasta: When it's al dente, drain it, keeping a little pasta water if needed.
  5. Season: Mix the pasta with butter or olive oil. If needed, add a bit of pasta water for a smoother texture.
  6. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese (optional) and enjoy!
